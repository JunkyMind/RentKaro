Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property through RentKaro?

To book a property, simply browse our listings, select a property you like, and click on the "Book Now" button. You'll need to create an account or log in, provide necessary details, and make a booking payment.

What documents do I need to rent a property?

Typically, you'll need a valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Driving License), college/university ID card, passport-sized photographs, and sometimes a guarantor's details or income proof.

How does the security deposit work?

Security deposits are typically 1-2 months' rent, paid at the beginning of your tenancy. They're refundable at the end of your stay, subject to any deductions for damages or outstanding bills.

Can I schedule a property visit before booking?

Yes, you can schedule a property visit through our website or by contacting our support team. We recommend visiting the property before making a final decision.
